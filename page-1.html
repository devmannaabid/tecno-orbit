Associate Business Development Managers

If you are passionate about tech and eager to drive growth, jump in as an Associate Business Development Manager and get hands-on experience with the latest tech innovations, while learning from the best in the game!

Responsibilities

Conduct market analysis to identify opportunities, monitor trends, competitor actions, and emerging business prospects, while regularly updating the lead database to ensure data integrity and support data-driven decision-making
Support communication efforts while collaborating with Sales, Marketing, and technology / Innovation teams to drive lead generation and strengthen brand presence.
Assisting to enhance N-able’s industry presence and thought leadership through company events, webinars and conferences.

Requirements

Currently pursuing a Bachelor’s Degree in Marketing, Management, or a related discipline. Exceptional written and verbal communication skills.
Proficient in conducting detailed online research.
Strong interest in how digital marketing strategies drive lead generation and contribute to business growth.Self-starter with a professional, outgoing demeanor and a talent for building meaningful relationships.
