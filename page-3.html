Senior Business Development Manager – Public Sector

N*able has a well-established track record of delivering transformative solutions to businesses in Sri Lanka, enabling them to evolve their operations and technology foundations. Within our strategic focus on several sectors, we are seeking a Senior Business Development Manager – Public Sector.

Responsibilities

  • A minimum prior experience of 5-10 years in a similar role is mandatory and an existing account portfolio in this sector would be an added advantage
  • Excellent interpersonal skills and able to relate to a wide group of technical and business leaders
  • Ability to tell a compelling story around technology to non-technical audiences
  • Ability to drive relevant stakeholders (both internal & external) with urgency to promote business opportunities from one stage to another with a relentless focus on winning and closing the opportunity.
  • Experience and exposure to key IT vendors and technologies
  • Strong negotiation skills across multiple stakeholders from commencement to closure
  • Must be able to highlight and demonstrate excellent follow up and communication skills
  • Proven track record of working on large-scale public-sector projects

About N-able

At N*able, our philosophy centers around attracting top talent and empowering them to excel in their areas of expertise. We believe in granting our team members the autonomy to lead projects, exercise sound judgment and leverage their innovative ideas to propel the company forward. Continuous learning and skill expansion are core values, whilst our professionals receive training from industry-leading veterans.
We believe in getting the best people and letting them do what they’re best at. We offer flexibility to lead your projects, and an open and collaborative environment.
