1 April 2025

N-able is pleased to announce the appointment of Shirantha Herath as its new Chief Executive Officer. Shirantha brings a wealth of industry expertise and leadership experience, having held pivotal roles across diverse sectors.

Shirantha has been a key member of N-able’s leadership, having served as a Director at N-able where he contributed significantly to shaping its strategic direction. He brings over 32 years of experience in leading a diverse set of private and state sector organizations. As Director General at the Presidential Secretariat from 2022 to 2024, Shirantha played a pivotal role in coordinating the diverse initiatives towards the economic recovery – including coordination of the IMF program for the Chief of Staff.

His deep familiarity with N-able’s vision and operations, combined with his extensive experience, makes him uniquely positioned to lead the company through its next phase of growth.

“We are pleased to welcome Shirantha as N-able’s new CEO. His proven leadership, extensive experience and intimate knowledge of N-able make him the ideal choice to lead the next phase of N-able journey,” stated Buddhike Illangatillake, Chairman & Managing Director of N-able.

Shirantha expressed his enthusiasm, stating, “I am honored to lead N-able into its next phase of growth. I look forward to working with the team to deliver impactful solutions that empower our clients to thrive in the current technology landscape.”

Shirantha succeeds Asanka Bimal Rajasinghe, whose leadership played a key role in N-able’s journey over the past 17 years. With this leadership transition, N-able remains committed to its mission of delivering impactful solutions and driving business value for clients while upholding the values that have defined the company since its inception.
