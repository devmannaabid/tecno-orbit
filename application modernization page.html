Application modernization & integration

For your business to innovate, your channels of customer interaction and the supporting back-office must be agile, adaptive and provide space for rapid innovation.

To realize these ends, you need to modernize and modernize your applications and the way they integrate with each other in unison. To realize the full benefits of modernization, you must also transform your application delivery pipelines through automation and bring increased observability to your modernized applications.

Our Solutions

Innovation is the new fuel for business growth. To innovate faster, your applications must be agile, adaptive and extensible. This requires a holistic approach to aligning your applications into fine grained, independently manageable units of business logic that can evolve and scale on their own rapidly and resiliently.

Our modernization architects and engineers can help you to
  • Plan your application modernization journey starting with low hanging fruits to more challenges monoliths.
  • Guide you through establishing automation pipelines, monitoring and governance capabilities and security architecture to succeed in your modernization efforts.
  • Using several modern techniques, break and migrate your monolithic application architectures into business specific functional micro-apps.
  • Ensure your modernized application landscape utilizes the full benefits of modern consumption-based cloud delivery models. icon

Traditional patterns of application integration such as centralized ESB (Enterprise Service Bus) pattern have had their limitations exposed in modern microservice-driven agile enterprise application landscapes. Most often, bottlenecks emerge in projects due to the emphasis on re-use, synchronous communication styles and lack of decentralized administration.

In contrast, modern integration architectures tend to align more with your containerized microservice applications providing dynamic scalability and resilience while ensuring adaptability.

Our integration architects, powered with years of experience in designing futurist integration architecture for banking-grade/carrier-grade application landscapes, can help you to,
  • Design fine-grained micro-integrations aligned to your application modernization goals.
  • Deploy and support such integrations in cloud native runtimes that your different teams and own and independently evolve.
  • Boosting your ability to respond to rapidly changing business demands.

Business APIs fuel innovation by providing you with avenues to complement and enhance your business capabilities by collaborating with your partners and suppliers in ways that were never possible before. To accelerate progress in your business aligned API journey, you must securely expose business APIs that are partner or supplier centric. To achieve this, you need to build capacity to orchestrate complex, domain specific System APIs from your backend applications.

Whether your goal is to monetize your business capabilities through API’s or to build new business models through innovative vertical or horizonal business integrations, our API architects and API builders can help you to,
  • Plan and design your API journey to drive new business models you discover with your partners and suppliers.
  • Design, develop and support business API’s and build security and governance around them to help you realize the API-centric business models.
